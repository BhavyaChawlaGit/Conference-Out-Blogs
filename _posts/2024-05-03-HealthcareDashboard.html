Introduction

In the rapidly evolving field of healthcare technology, data visualization is essential for enhancing operational efficiency and patient care. This blog outlines the development of a comprehensive healthcare dashboard that tracks patient waiting lists from 2018 to 2021, distinguishing between inpatient and outpatient data.

Project Background

The aim was to build a dashboard to help healthcare professionals gain insights into patient flow and resource allocation efficiency over several years. Utilizing Power BI, I crafted visualizations that provide both operational insights and support strategic decision-making.

Project Goals and Data Scope

Project Goals:

  • Track the current status of the patient waiting list: Ensuring real-time monitoring of patient wait times.
  • Analyze historical monthly trends of waiting lists: Distinguishing between inpatient and outpatient services.
  • Conduct detailed specialty-level and age profile analysis: Offering deeper insights into specific service areas and patient demographics.

Data Scope:
This project used data spanning from 2018 to 2021, covering various aspects of hospital patient management systems.

Metrics and Views Required

Metrics:

  • Average and Median Waiting List: To measure central tendencies of waiting times across different categories.
  • Current Total Wait List: For real-time status checks on all patients waiting for services.

Views Required:

  • Summary Page: For high-level overviews and quick insights.
  • Detailed Page for Granular Analysis: For detailed explorations of data by specialists.
