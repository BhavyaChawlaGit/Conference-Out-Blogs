Hi, I am a red-eared turtle and I am a year old and you can tell I'll take time to grow.

I was found in CHInatown, CHIcago and I got a really awesome energy around me, CHI energy

But I am here to share my thoughts and experiences as well as my journey to grow.

Let's grow together.

My journey as a Chi started on August 27, 2023. I am not sure what these three random guys were up to, but I am sure I wasn't on their shopping list

That's baby me, being rescued! Well can't say that tbh and in fact, I can't talk.

So that's me being carried away from a shop to somewhere I wasn't sure about. And so many questions I had.

  1. Will it turn out well for me? - At least I am alive.
  2. Will I be fed properly and thrice a day? - Hmmm yeah these bachelors don't cook for me but got me loads of shrimps, can't complain
  3. Will I have a better place to live? - These guys themselves dont have one so will keep this for later

So many questions and so many thoughts. But I am here now and I am happy. I am growing and I am happy. I am loved and I am happy. I am happy and I am happy.


What's next?

I love basking, something new that I discovered, always used to think why are these humans lying naked on the beach? Nope I have never been to a beach but I once saw my human googling about it, in fact, all the time for the naked ones!

So I easily get to spend a couple of hours basking, stretching my legs, and ending it with sun-dried shrimp and obviously, I go to sleep after that.

I know I know you lo-key jealous but my life was not always like this

Small me, small container, small hopes of surviving, and just watching humans walk past, getting excited looking at me and I used to wonder whether it would be love or some marination falling over me

I am happy now and I am growing. I am loved and I am happy.

So let's grow together and see where this journey takes us.

Stay tuned for more updates on my journey.

CHI
